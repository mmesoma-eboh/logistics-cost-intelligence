## Breakthrough Insights

### The "Two-Stream" Cost Discovery

**Problem**: "Total Cost" was misleading, masking operational efficiency.

**Solution**: Separated costs into:
- **Core Freight (68%)**: Predictable, controllable costs suitable for accurate budgeting
- **Volatile Costs (32%)**: Service fees, anomalies, accounting noise requiring management attention

**Impact**: Operational efficiency was being hidden by accounting classification.

### NYC-FRA Consolidation Opportunity

**Finding**: 8 shipments for just 40kg of freight at $296/kg (3-4x industry norm)

**Root Cause**: Poor consolidation practices and small shipment inefficiencies

**Solution**: Lane-specific consolidation policy with minimum weight requirements

**Financial Impact**: 60%+ cost reduction, $75K+ annual savings

### Financial Forecasting Enhancement

**Methodology**: Driver-based forecasting using operational cost drivers

**Improvement**: 25%+ accuracy improvement over traditional budgeting methods

**Business Value**: Enables proactive P&L management and variance reduction
